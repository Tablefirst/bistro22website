Our Story Accra’s Favourite

Kitchen + Bar

Since opening our doors in 2014, Bistro 22 has become one of Accra’s most beloved dining destinations — a place where great food, handcrafted cocktails, and genuine hospitality come together in the heart of Labone.

We bring the world’s kitchen to Ghana, curating a menu that weaves international flavour with local soul. Whether you’re settling in for a long weekend lunch, catching up over cocktails, or celebrating a special evening, Bistro 22 sets the scene.

Recognised with the Ghana Tourism Award, our reputation is built on consistent quality, warm service, and an atmosphere that makes every visit feel effortless and memorable.
